Amazing Facts About Rabbits

Introducing Rabbits :

1. Rabbits are gentle and social animals that enjoy staying in groups.
2. They are most active during early mornings and evenings.
3. Rabbits love to dig, chew, and explore their surroundings for fun and safety.
4. They have excellent hearing and can sense danger quickly.
